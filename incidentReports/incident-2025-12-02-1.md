# Incident: 2025-12-02 08-00-00 
## Summary
Between the hour of 8 am and 9 am on December 2, 2025, several users encoutered an issue with ordering pizzas. The event was triggered by Chaos Monkey and caused an issue with the connection to the pizza factory. The event was detected by Grafana. I started working on the event by looking at the logs as well as the metrics on Grafana until I found the link to stop the Chaos.

## Detection
This incident was detected when the Grafana alert was triggered and Sam was emailed.

## Impact
For 0 hrs 15 minutes between 15:00 UTC and 15:15 UTC on 12/02/25, users were unable to order pizzas because of an issue with the pizza factory.

This incident affected 10 customers, who experienced failer in the order pizza endpoint.

0 tickets were submitted.

## Timeline
All times are UTC

- _15:00_ - Chaos started
- _15:04_ - Grafana alert sent to Sam Roberts
- _15:10_ - Sam started looking at the logs
- _15:15_ - Link found in the logs and issued resolved

## Response
After receiving an email at 15:04 UTC, Sam Roberts came online at 15:10 UTC in Grafana.

This engineer was able to diagnose and resolve the issue.

## Root Cause
Chaos Monkey caused the connection to the pizza factory to stop working.

## Resolution
Disabled the chaos and the endpoint came back up.

## Prevention
Ensure connection to pizza factory doesn't break.
